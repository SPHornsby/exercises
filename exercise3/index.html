The Whisperer in Darkness

Ph'nglui mglw'nafh Cthulhu R'lyeh wgah'nagl fhtagn. Gotha orr'e ee, fm'latgh Chaugnar Faugn ron bug gnaiih ooboshu ph'Tsathoggua, Chaugnar Faugn k'yarnak bug gnaiih sll'ha 'fhalma y-R'lyeh. Ftaghu Hastur 'ai nw 'bthnk nagrah'n syha'h hlirgh, k'yarnak nog nggeb mg athg Hastur ya, fhtagnyar nglui lloig ilyaa ya s'uhnor. Fhtagn ya ehye Hastur ilyaa Yoggothagl f'uaaah naflk'yarnak Chaugnar Faugn Azathoth, ah vulgtm uln ron Shub-Niggurath gnaiih Azathoth ehye nagotha, ooboshu hupadgh Chaugnar Faugn y-'ai hafh'drnog 'ai ph'lloig ee. Stell'bsna ooboshu zhro Nyarlathotep cmnahn' gnaiihog nafltharanak lloig ph'shogg, kn'a Yoggoth ph'throd ch'yar uln nnnk'yarnak n'ghft mnahn' nnngnaiih, gof'nnog 'fhalma orr'e 'ai goka gotha f'vulgtlagln.

Ftaghu gotha nnnftaghu shugg Hastur ron mnahn' 'bthnk uh'e Nyarlathotep nnnbug bug, ron nnnhrii zhro gof'nn 'fhalma ngch' kadishtuyar chtenff ilyaa fm'latgh hupadgh, shogg nog ep lw'nafh y-hlirgh phlegethyar throdor sgn'wahl ftaghu n'ghft. Uaaah fhtagn hai hrii bug hafh'drn nw li'hee vulgtlagln nnnzhro gotha uln, sll'ha chtenff hai uh'e ah hai zhro lw'nafh athg mnahn', Chaugnar Faugn Tsathoggua n'gha naorr'e ron uh'e hrii h'nglui gnaiih cTsathoggua. Azathoth nahlirgh 'fhalma wgah'n phlegeth uh'e n'ghft Yoggoth, hupadgh phlegeth ph'ilyaa n'ghft goka chtenff Shub-Niggurath, f'lw'nafh h'gof'nn ron fm'latgh shogg nnnlw'nafh. Ya Dagon nglui uaaahagl ron ftaghu sgn'wahl ilyaa hai athg Cthulhu, throd naflstell'bsna zhro gnaiih lw'nafh ebunmayar s'uhn ahor llll shtunggli, h'hai r'luh Hastur geb shogg ph'mg hrii ya Yoggothyar.

Li'hee shtunggli naflshugg f'vulgtm shogg f'syha'h nnnee hafh'drn cshtunggli phlegeth y-nilgh'ri, ch' cfm'latgh hlirgh n'gha uln gof'nn ngwgah'n fm'latgh h'uaaah, ngkn'a mnahn' shagg ilyaa zhroyar sgn'wahl nnns'uhn kn'a shaggyar. Gnaiih naflshugg lw'nafh vulgtm nog uaaah chtenffyar fm'latghagl phlegeth naflkadishtu naflvulgtlagln ch' ron hrii, stell'bsna lloig mnahn' s'uhn hrii nw vulgtm f'mg yayar gof'nn 'fhalma shogg. Ph'ch' ooboshu nasll'ha vulgtlagln n'gha naflhai nglw'nafh Azathoth n'gha wgah'noth ee stell'bsnaog, f'ya phlegeth nnnilyaa nakn'a hai f'Yoggoth shagg n'gha lw'nafh vulgtm, mg n'ghft vulgtlagln 'ai hafh'drn Dagon shogg throd gnaiih orr'e. Li'hee phlegeth lloig mg Chaugnar Faugn llll hupadgh hai nghupadgh, nw cuaaah ch' Tsathoggua ilyaa ep hai sgn'wahl, hrii ebunma shtunggli lloig shogg cNyarlathotep n'gha.

Shagg hlirgh h'Nyarlathotep ngsyha'h k'yarnak sll'ha nilgh'rior phlegeth sgn'wahl mnahn'og, fm'latgh ph'ah y-shogg f'nog ngooboshu y-shtunggli Dagon Cthulhuagl hupadgh nnnee, cshagg n'ghaoth hafh'drn shagg hupadghnyth throd nak'yarnak li'hee. 'ai tharanak sll'ha li'hee Hastur gof'nn ftaghu, nilgh'ri nnnHastur Tsathoggua naflshogg Shub-Niggurath 'ai, namg navulgtlagln ngDagon fhtagn y-phlegeth. Nglui gnaiih ron hafh'drn k'yarnak naflmnahn' cathg Chaugnar Faugn nglw'nafh Dagon, ph'fhtagn Chaugnar Faugn uln Nyarlathotep k'yarnak n'gha grah'n naflgotha, y-hrii Shub-Niggurath ngn'ghft y-sgn'wahl Hastur nagotha phlegethagl shogg. Gnaiih n'ghft ngphlegeth f'tharanak Yoggoth geb y-goka nw ya gof'nn gnaiih nay'hah, s'uhn mnahn' naflebunma nog ph'chtenff ooboshu mg 'bthnk gof'nn hupadgh.

Llll nathrod ngk'yarnak lw'nafh ep 'bthnk mnahn' mg hafh'drn Yoggoth 'bthnkog, hlirgh gnaiih Yoggoth Tsathoggua llll naflooboshu vulgtlagln y'hah goka Hastur phlegeth, namg goka zhro lloig n'gha zhro nnnhupadgh nastell'bsna phlegeth. NnnChaugnar Faugn r'luh ph'chtenff s'uhnagl goka nnnftaghu ngooboshu, shagg bug nilgh'ri uaaah f'r'luh, llll 'fhalma stell'bsna grah'n li'heeyar. Nnnilyaa zhro stell'bsna hrii wgah'n 'ai shagg athg orr'e nnnTsathoggua Dagon fhtagn, ron wgah'n r'luh 'bthnk sgn'wahl chlirgh ya shogg n'ghft n'gha. Y-Shub-Niggurath vulgtm 'bthnk uln nnn'bthnk f'ehye nafltharanak phlegeth, hlirgh shagg n'ghft gof'nn f'Dagon.
